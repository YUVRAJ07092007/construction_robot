# Important Video Links for Video-Informed Data Generation in Construction Robot Deployment Research

## Purpose of This Document

This document identifies and reviews important video and technical source links suitable for the proposed research paper on:

**Video-Informed and GAN-Augmented Framework for Assessing Construction Robot Deployment Readiness in Aluminium Formwork-Based High-Rise Building Construction**

The videos are divided into two categories:

1. **BrightMaster / construction robot videos** — for extracting robot-side parameters.
2. **Mivan / aluminium formwork construction videos** — for extracting construction workflow-side parameters.

These videos should be used as **video-derived observational sources**, not as direct proof of productivity or real-site measured performance.

# G. Recommended Wording for the Paper

Use the following wording to protect the methodology from reviewer criticism:

> The selected videos were used to extract observable task-level parameters and workflow characteristics. Manufacturer pages were used only to define technical specification ranges. The video-derived dataset was not treated as direct field-measured productivity data, but as a structured secondary observational dataset for framework development and synthetic scenario generation.

This wording is important because it clearly separates:

1. **Video-derived observational data**
2. **Manufacturer-reported specifications**
3. **Synthetic scenario generation**
4. **Actual field-measured productivity data**

The study should not claim real-site validation unless such field data are actually collected.
